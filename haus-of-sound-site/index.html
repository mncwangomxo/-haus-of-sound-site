Haus of Sound

Haus of Sound is a cultural experience platform that curates premium, sound-led events across South Africa. Blending sonic atmosphere with visual finesse, we craft unforgettable moments that linger long after the beat fades.

Born from a desire to elevate the everyday party into a moment of artistic resonance, we transform venues into immersive storyboards where sound, scent, and styling become a unified narrative.

Our branded activations fuse substance with style — creating intimate showcases and bold large-scale moments grounded in impact, authenticity, and sensory design.

About Haus of Sound

Founded by visionary creative director Mxolisi, Haus of Sound is South Africa’s leading sonic curator — a platform where music, visual artistry, and immersive ambiance collide to create unforgettable cultural experiences.

We specialize in branded activations and experiential design that seamlessly fuse style with substance. Whether hosting intimate showcases or electrifying large-scale events, our approach is rooted in impact, authenticity, and sensory exploration.

More than an event brand, we are building a movement — one that celebrates collaboration, creativity, and culture.

Our Mission

To create sonic sanctuaries and stylistic environments that move people — not just emotionally, but culturally. Haus of Sound exists to define a new kind of nightlife: purposeful, beautiful, and unapologetically South African.

Our mission is to unite cultures and nations through the shared language of sound — to deepen the purpose of music, expand its reach, and celebrate collective connection. We believe that through music, space transforms, and people find each other.

This is where you SIP, DANCE & REPEAT.
